Pink pasta

Description

PinkPasta is one of the most delicious recipe you would ever try!, it's yummy and easy to do.

It's very simple and doesn't need a lot of ingredients, don't worry! won't take a lot of time :).

ingredients

  • pasta
  • Chicken breast
  • Mushroom
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Cooking cream
  • Hot red pepper
  • Spices
    • Salt
    • Black pepper
    • Oregano
    • Onion powder
    • Garlic powder
    • Ginger

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Add the spices to your chicken cubes
  3. Cock the chicken for 10 minutes
  4. Add one tablespoon of Tomato paste
  5. Put half-cup of water and mix it all over
  6. Add cooking cream
  7. Mix it all over for couple of minutes
  8. Add a lot of Mozarella cheese :)

That's it ! Enjoy your meal.
